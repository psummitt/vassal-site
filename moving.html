Vassal is moving to a new host!

We're outgrown the hosting provider we've had since 2009. We're presently copying data from our old host to our new host, where we'll have a more reliable connection to the internet and three times the outgoing bandwidth.

During this process, the main site will be inaccessible, but the game server will still be available. We expect to be up and running at our new host within a day or so.
